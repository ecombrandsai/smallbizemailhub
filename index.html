Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best email marketing software for small business in 2026?

After testing more than a dozen platforms, our editorial team's top pick is Constant Contact. It combines a 60-day free trial, hands-on phone support, modern templates, and predictable $12/month entry pricing that small businesses can plan around.

Is email marketing still effective in 2026?

Yes — average ROI for small-business email marketing is $36 returned for every $1 spent, according to multiple industry benchmark studies. Unlike social platforms, you own the audience and the channel, so a single algorithm change can't wipe out your reach.

How much should a small business pay for email marketing?

Most small businesses we work with spend between $12 and $80 per month depending on list size. At entry pricing, even a single conversion per month covers the cost — and a typical small list of 500 contacts generates 1-3 attributable sales per send.

Do I need a website to start email marketing?

No. Most email tools, including our top pick Constant Contact, include free landing pages and signup forms you can link to from your Google Business Profile, Instagram bio, business cards, or POS. A website helps, but isn't required to launch your first list.

How fast can I see results from email marketing?

Most small businesses see their first attributable sale within 30 days of their first campaign. The compounding effects — list growth, repeat-customer revenue, and automation-driven sales — typically become meaningful in the 60-90 day window.
