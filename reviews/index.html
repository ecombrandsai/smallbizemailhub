How we score reviews

Every review goes through the same testing methodology:

  • 90 days of hands-on usage on a paid plan
  • Real list (not test data) — minimum 500 contacts
  • Minimum 20 sends across the test window
  • 5 inbox monitors tracking deliverability (Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, Apple Mail, Yahoo Japan)
  • Multiple support calls, chats, and email contacts
  • Comparison baseline against an incumbent tool

Reviews are scored on seven weighted criteria: ease of use (20%), deliverability (20%), price (15%), support (15%), template quality (10%), automation depth (10%), and integration ecosystem (10%). See our methodology page for full details.
