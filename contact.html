What we love to hear about

  • Corrections. If we got a pricing tier wrong, missed a new feature, or a tool's policies changed — tell us. We update reviews monthly but real-world feedback is the fastest path to accuracy.
  • Tool requests. Want us to test a platform we don't currently cover? Send us the name and what your use case is.
  • Reader questions. Stuck picking between two tools? Trying to migrate? Confused about a pricing tier? Email us and we'll point you to the right resource.
  • Press inquiries. Journalists, podcasters, and authors — happy to chat about the state of email marketing for small business.

One thing we can't do

We can't give legal, financial, or compliance advice on your email marketing setup. We can help you pick a tool. We can't tell you whether your specific opt-in process is GDPR-compliant. If you have legal questions about email marketing in your jurisdiction, please consult a qualified attorney.
