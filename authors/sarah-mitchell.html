About Sarah

Sarah Mitchell is EmailToolAdviser's Senior Reviews Editor. She has covered email marketing software for small businesses for the last decade, with a focus on platform reviews and beginner-friendly guides. Sarah's reviews draw on public deliverability benchmark data (DMA, Litmus, the Email Sender & Provider Coalition) combined with clearly-labeled editorial scoring — a transparent approach that lets readers see exactly what is publicly-sourced fact and what is editorial opinion. She does not accept compensation tied to specific platform recommendations and partner influence has no impact on her ratings.

Editorial standards

Sarah Mitchell follows EmailToolAdviser's editorial standards: public benchmark data is cited with sources; editorial opinion is labeled as editorial opinion; first-party measurement claims are only made when first-party measurement was actually conducted (which is rarely the case for individual software platforms). Reader trust depends on this distinction.
